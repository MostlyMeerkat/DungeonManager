Reminders

  • Characters don't fail! The world is just unpredictable and dangerous.
  • Stealth is always an option for PCs as well as NPCs
  • LISTEN TO YOUR PLAYERS! DON'T ZONE OUT
  • Monsters/NPCs want to survive, not just mindlessly attack PCs
  • USE LEGENDARY ACTIONS AFTER A PC FINISHES THEIR TURN
  • Keep combat moving
  • Check your monsters' stat block for unique moves (or make some up in advance)
  • Ask questions to the players: How are you moving? How is ____ feeling?
